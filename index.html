Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the development of computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation. Machine Learning is a subset of AI that focuses on the development of algorithms and statistical models that enable computers to learn from and improve their performance on a specific task without being explicitly programmed.
