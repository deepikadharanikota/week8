The widespread adoption of artificial intelligence (AI) can have both positive and negative effects on humans. On the positive side, AI has the potential to enhance efficiency, improve productivity, and create new opportunities in various industries. However, there are concerns about job displacement as automation may replace certain roles, leading to unemployment and the need for reskilling. Additionally, ethical considerations related to privacy, bias in algorithms, and the potential misuse of AI technologies raise important questions about the impact of AI on societal well-being and human values.
